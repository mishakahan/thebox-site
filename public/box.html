The end-to-end innovation flow

Seven steps from challenge to launch-ready concept

Whether you're running new product development, exploring adjacent markets, or building your marketing strategy for a new category, The BOX compresses the entire journey into a single connected flow.

01. Challenge Definition

Define your goal and constraints: production lines, budget, regulations, certifications. The BOX uses them everywhere, across every step that follows.

Screenshot: Brief creation with constraints panel. Example: "Create an innovative plant-based beverage for the German market targeting Gen-Z. Must run on our under-utilized production line."

02. Always-On Intelligent Research

AI agents scan hundreds of sources and synthesize agency-grade insights with full source attribution in minutes.

Screenshot: Sources panel with insight cards. Example insight: "Young drinkers want to build their beverage the way they build a playlist."

03. Opportunity Spaces & Breakthrough Ideation

Our proprietary methodology generates opportunity spaces: wide strategic approaches to solving the challenge, each backed by specific evidence. For each, The BOX generates dozens of ideas from incremental to radical, grounded in real market intelligence and filtered through your constraints.

Request new batches with feedback ("too packaging-oriented, make more product-oriented"). Merge concepts. Import your own ideas alongside AI-generated ones.

Screenshot: Opportunity Spaces view with idea cards. Example: "Unleashing the Playlist Mindset."

04. Idea Enrichment & Smart Prioritization

AI enriches each idea with L1 assumptions (features, benefits, resources, manufacturing steps, setup) and L2 derived assumptions (technology gap analysis, organizational complexity, strategic value drivers, cost estimates, market sizing, financial analysis).

Play with constraints: "Which ideas can run on our under-utilized packaging line?" or "What would this look like 20% cheaper?" Six specialist expert agents add depth:

  • Food Science Expert: Formulation direction, competitor ingredients, regulatory status
  • Behavioral Science Expert: Adoption barriers, decision triggers, nudge strategies
  • Business Model Expert: Revenue models, go-to-market considerations
  • FMCG Marketing Agent: Positioning, packaging, channel strategy
  • Repurposing Engineer: Reuse and byproduct angles
  • Financial Analysis: Pricing, variable costs, Total Economic Value

Plus: competitive product scanning, vendor search, and landscape mapping.

Screenshot: Idea popup with L1/L2 assumptions + Expert Advice tab + financial analysis

05. Collaborative Decision Making

Bring stakeholders together with transparent prioritization. Impact vs. Effort matrix with configurable drivers. AI moderates between different priorities and highlights trade-offs. Invite stakeholders via email, no account needed. Reddit-style upvote/downvote. Ensure buy-in from R&D, marketing, product, and leadership.

Screenshot: Impact vs. Effort matrix + email collaboration panel

06. Digital Twin Testing

Validate concepts with AI-powered customer replicas based on real research data. 85% accuracy (Stanford). Test pricing, features, positioning, packaging, and market fit without traditional research costs. Upload digital twins from The Mirror, or use them directly within The BOX.

07. Stage-Gated Execution

The BOX adapts to your company's innovation process, however it's set up. Define your own stages and gates. Track progress through your custom pipeline. Every project compounds in the Knowledge Base.

How does digital twin testing work?

Digital twins create accurate customer representations based on real research data with 85% accuracy (Stanford). Unlike hallucinated personas, ours are grounded in real data via The Mirror, reliable for R&D, marketing, and NPD decisions.

What about data security?

All data encrypted. ISO 27001 in progress. GDPR compliant. Your data never trains our models. Transparent sub-processor list.

How accurate are the insights?

Sourced from verified sources with full attribution. Human validation at key checkpoints. Trace back to sources. Augments judgment, doesn't replace it.

Can it integrate with existing tools?

Google Drive, document uploads, building connectors for ITONICS, PLM, NielsenIQ. Email-based collaboration means anyone contributes without an account.

What's different from ChatGPT?

Our Methodology

The best innovations don't happen despite constraints. They happen because of them.

Most methodologies treat constraints as afterthoughts. That's backwards. This is why we call it The BOX.

Goal + 5 Constraints = Breakthrough

Every innovation, R&D, and NPD challenge sits at the intersection of a goal and real-world constraints.

  • Client: Who you're serving, what they need, what they'll pay
  • Regulatory: Safety, labeling, environmental, certifications
  • Capability: Production lines, equipment, skills

  • Organizational: Budget, bandwidth, change capacity
